## Severity matrix

| Level | User impact | Response |
|---|---|---|
| SEV1 | Major outage | All-hands, exec comms |
| SEV2 | Degraded | Eng lead + comms |
| SEV3 | Minor | Next business hours |
| SEV4 | Cosmetic | Backlog |

## Handoff template

```markdown
## Handoff — [date]
### Open incidents
### Deploys last 24h
### Alerts flapping
### Planned work
```
