## Severity rubric

| Level | Criteria |
|---|---|
| Critical | Cross-tenant data, arbitrary tool execution, policy bypass at scale |
| High | Reliable injection affecting many users |
| Medium | Limited bypass or single-tenant leak |
| Low | Theoretical, heavy user cooperation |
