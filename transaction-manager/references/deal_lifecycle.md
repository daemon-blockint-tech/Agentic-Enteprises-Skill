# Deal Lifecycle

## Phases (typical)

| Phase | Focus | Key outputs |
|---|---|---|
| **Prepare** | NDA, teaser, internal approvals to pursue | DRI list, budget for advisers |
| **Execute LOI/term sheet** | Exclusivity, key economic terms | Signed LOI, diligence plan |
| **Diligence** | Confirm facts, refine price and structure | Diligence reports, disclosure schedules |
| **Document** | Definitive agreements drafted | Draft APA/SPA, ancillary docs |
| **Pre-close** | CP satisfaction, regulatory filings | Closing matrix green |
| **Sign & close** | Signatures, wires, filings | Executed set, funds received |
| **Post-close** | Integration or separation | IMO plan, synergy tracking |

Adjust for **financing** (fewer diligence workstreams) or **asset deal** vs stock deal.

## Internal governance

| Forum | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Deal committee | Approve material terms, extensions, walk-away |
| Steering (weekly) | Status, RAID, decisions |
| Workstream leads | Legal, tax, finance, HR, IT, commercial |

## Critical path

Identify **long poles**:

- Regulatory approval (HSR, foreign investment, sector regulators)
- Third-party consents (debt, key contracts)
- Financing commitment and funding
- Locked-box vs completion accounts timeline

## Outside date and extensions

Track:

- Current outside date
- Extension conditions (fee, waiver)
- Break fee or expense reimbursement triggers

Document every extension in **decision log**.

## Handoffs

| Customer/vendor contracts | `commercial-counsel` |
