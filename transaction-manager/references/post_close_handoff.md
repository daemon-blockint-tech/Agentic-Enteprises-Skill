# Post-Close Handoff

## Immediate (T+0 to T+5 days)

| Item | Owner |
|---|---|
| Announcement (if not pre-close) | `communication-lead` |
| Customer / vendor notices | Commercial + legal |
| Systems access and email | IT |
| Payroll and benefits mapping | `people-operations-specialist` |
| Banking and treasury | Finance |
| Press hold / leak response plan | Comms + deal lead |

## Integration management office (IMO)

Charter should define:

- **Scope** — functions, geographies, legal entities
- **Governance** — IMO lead, exec sponsor, workstreams
- **Synergy targets** — cost and revenue (from `business-consultant` case)
- **Cadence** — weekly IMO, monthly exec

Hand program execution to `technical-program-manager` when work is multi-team technical delivery.

## Day-one readiness

| Workstream | Day-one must-haves |
|---|---|
| IT | Identity, network, critical apps |
| HR | Reporting lines, payroll cutover |
| Finance | Chart of accounts mapping, controls |
| Legal | Contract notices, IP assignments |
| Sales | Quota and territory (if applicable) |

## 30 / 60 / 90

| Horizon | Focus |
|---|---|
| **30** | Stabilize operations, quick wins, no-regret decisions |
| **60** | Deeper integration, tool consolidation plans |
| **90** | Synergy reporting, org design finalization |

## Separation (divestiture)

Mirror with **TSA** (transition services agreement):

- Services catalog, duration, pricing
- Exit criteria and run-down
- Separate closing matrix for carve-out CPs

## Deal folder archive

- Executed agreements, FFM, closing certificates
- Final diligence reports
- Decision log and steering minutes
- Retention per legal hold policy
