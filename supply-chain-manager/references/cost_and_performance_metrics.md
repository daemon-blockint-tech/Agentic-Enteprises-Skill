## Total cost view

**Landed cost** includes:

- Unit price
- Freight, duties, insurance
- Quality cost (scrap, rework, returns)
- Inventory carrying cost
- Expedite premium
- Obsolescence write-down

Compare suppliers on **TCO**, not invoice price alone.

## Should-cost and savings

Methods:

- **Should-cost** model from BOM, labor, materials index
- **Benchmark** peer quotes and market indices
- **VA/VE** workshops with engineering
- **PPV** (purchase price variance) vs standard

Track savings **validated in P&L** — finance confirms, not procurement self-score only.

Hardware invoice classification → `compute-accounting-manager` for GL, not sourcing strategy.
