## Rollout steps

1. Final text approved (HR + counsel for material changes)
2. Summary of what changed for managers
3. Employee notification with effective date
4. Acknowledgment window and reminders
5. Archive prior version; update intranet

## Acknowledgment

Track per employee: policy version, date, method (click-wrap, e-sign).
Report outstanding acknowledgments weekly until closed.
