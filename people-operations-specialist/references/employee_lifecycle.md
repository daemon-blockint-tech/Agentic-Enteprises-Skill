## Stages

| Stage | People ops focus |
|---|---|
| Pre-hire | Offer entered, background, right-to-work |
| Onboard | HRIS, accounts, policies, equipment |
| Active | Changes (transfer, promotion), leave, reviews |
| Leave | LOA coordination with payroll and manager |
| Offboard | Access, assets, final pay, alumni status |

Route compensation philosophy and executive hiring to HR leadership + counsel as required.
