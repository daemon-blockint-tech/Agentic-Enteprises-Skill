## Onboarding checklist

- [ ] HRIS profile created
- [ ] Offer letter and I-9 (or local equivalent) complete
- [ ] Payroll and benefits enrollment
- [ ] IT: email, SSO, laptop, required groups
- [ ] Manager: day-one agenda, buddy assigned
- [ ] Policy acknowledgments recorded
- [ ] Security training assigned

## Offboarding checklist

- [ ] Separation date confirmed in HRIS
- [ ] IT ticket: revoke access at scheduled time
- [ ] Recover equipment and badges
- [ ] Forward or delegate email per policy
- [ ] Payroll: final wages, PTO payout per jurisdiction
- [ ] Benefits termination / COBRA notice triggered
- [ ] Exit survey or interview scheduled if applicable
