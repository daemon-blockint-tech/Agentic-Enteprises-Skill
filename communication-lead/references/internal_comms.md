## All-hands structure

| Block | Time | Content |
|---|---|---|
| Context | 5 min | Why we're here; link to strategy |
| Results | 10 min | Metrics that matter; celebrate teams |
| Priorities | 10 min | Next quarter focus; what we stop |
| Deep dive | 10 min | One topic only |
| Q&A | 10 min | Live; pre-collect anonymous questions |

**Slides:** insight titles, not topic labels. **Recording:** note retention policy.

## Change communications

Sequence:

1. **Leaders** — full picture, talking points, anticipated objections
2. **Managers** — FAQ, how to answer team questions
3. **Company** — what changes, when, support resources
4. **Follow-up** — office hours, pulse survey if major change

Address: what's changing, what's not, timeline, where to get help.

## Manager cascade kit

Provide:

- Email template (copy-paste with `[local detail]` slots)
- FAQ (10–15 questions)
- Escalation path for HR/legal/sensitive topics
- Do-not-share list (M&A, personnel, unreleased product)

Never announce individual performance or disciplinary actions in company-wide channels.
