## Pyramid updates

Lead with the **so what** in the first line:

> **Recommend:** Delay GA one week to complete security review. **Impact:** Reduces launch risk; shifts revenue ~$X. **Ask:** Approve new date by EOD Friday.

Then: context, options considered, next steps.

For analytical depth (issue trees, models), use `business-consultant` references; this skill owns **wording and cadence**.

## Exec brief template

| Section | Max length |
|---|---|
| Headline | 1 sentence |
| Status | Green / yellow / red + one line why |
| Decisions needed | Bullets with owner and deadline |
| Top risks | 3 max |
| Wins | 2–3 bullets |
| Appendix link | Optional detail |

## Alignment without overload

- One **source of truth** doc per initiative; link from Slack/email
- Differentiate **FYI** vs **action required** in subject lines
- Pre-wire controversial messages with 1:1s before group send
- Record decisions in writing within 24h after meetings
