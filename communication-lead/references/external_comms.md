## Customer messaging

**Email structure:**

1. Subject — outcome or action (not internal codename)
2. Opening — who it's for and why now
3. What changed — bullets; link to docs
4. What they should do — one primary CTA
5. Support — how to get help

**In-app:** short headline + link; match email facts.

**Changelog:** factual; version/date; breaking changes first.

## Channel checklist

Before send:

- [ ] Product/engineering sign-off on accuracy
- [ ] Support briefed with macro and ticket tags
- [ ] Sales talk track aligned (no stronger promises than email)
- [ ] Legal review if pricing, data, compliance, or forward-looking
- [ ] Localization plan if non-English markets material

## Coordination RACI

| Activity | Comms lead | Product | Legal | Support |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Message draft | R/A | C | C | I |
| Factual accuracy | C | A | I | C |
| Send approval | A | C | C | I |
| Post-send FAQ | R | C | I | A |

R = responsible, A = accountable, C = consulted, I = informed.

For contract terms in notices, involve `commercial-counsel`.
