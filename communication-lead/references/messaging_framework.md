## Audience map

| Audience | Cares about | Channel | Success signal |
|---|---|---|---|
| Executives | Risk, ROI, decisions | Brief, deck | Decision logged |
| Employees | Job impact, clarity | All-hands, Slack | Fewer repeat questions |
| Customers | Reliability, value | Email, status page | Churn/support volume stable |
| Partners | Roadmap, commitments | Partner brief | No surprise escalations |

## Message house

```
        [One narrative — 2 sentences max]
    ┌──────────┬──────────┬──────────┐
    │ Pillar 1 │ Pillar 2 │ Pillar 3 │
    └──────────┴──────────┴──────────┘
         proof    proof    proof
```

Each pillar: one claim + one proof point. Drop pillars that overlap.

## Proof and attribution

- Use only verified metrics and dates
- Quotes: name, title, approval on file
- Forward-looking statements: legal review
- Competitor mentions: factual, sourced

## Tone and voice

| Situation | Tone |
|---|---|
| Growth / launch | Confident, specific, customer-outcome led |
| Change / reorg | Empathetic, transparent on what is known |
| Incident | Calm, factual, action-oriented |
| Bad news | Direct, no burying; include next step |

Avoid: jargon stacks, passive voice for accountability, hype without substance.
