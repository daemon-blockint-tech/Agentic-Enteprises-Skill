## Purpose and boundaries

Executive and board stories drive **decisions** under uncertainty. They are not:

- Full financial models (attach or hand off to FP&A / `business-consultant`)
- Legal disclosures (counsel-owned)
- Operational runbooks (`tech-writer-researcher`)

Success = audience knows **what you recommend, why, risks, and what you need from them**.

## Pyramid structure

```
APEX     — Recommendation + ask (one sentence)
TIER 2   — 3 key messages (why now, why us, why this option)
TIER 3   — Evidence per KM (metrics, cases, benchmarks)
BASE     — Appendix (methods, detail, sensitivity)
```

**Time-box:**

| Duration | Deliver |
|---|---|
| 3 min | Apex + 3 KMs |
| 10 min | Apex + KMs + top evidence |
| 30 min | Full pyramid + discussion on risks |

## Strategy narrative arc

For portfolio, market entry, or transformation choices:

1. **Where we play** — market, segment, capability (shared context)
2. **Why change** — external shift or performance gap (tension)
3. **Strategic choice** — explicit option selected (turn)
4. **How we win** — moat, capabilities, investments (resolution path)
5. **What we need** — capital, hires, partnerships (ask)

Show **rejected options** briefly to prove rigor—one line each with kill reason.

## Pitch and investment stories

**Pitch story** (internal or external) follows:

| Beat | Content |
|---|---|
| Problem | Who hurts, how much, why now |
| Insight | Why incumbents fail |
| Solution | What you do differently (demo optional) |
| Traction | Proof (metrics, logos, pilots) |
| Model | How you make money (high level) |
| Team | Why this team |
| Ask | Specific raise, use of funds, milestones |

Avoid hype adjectives; every claim needs **evidence or labeled assumption**.

Pair with `business-model-researcher` or `business-consultant` for market sizing depth.

## Product narrative

Connect **user problem → product choice → measurable outcome**:

```
For [user], who [job-to-be-done],
[product] provides [outcome]
unlike [alternative]
because [differentiator].
```

Roadmap stories: **now / next / later** with outcomes per horizon—not feature laundry lists.

Coordinate ship dates with engineering and `communication-lead` for external promises.

## Objection handling

Pre-write **top 5 objections** for exec/board:

| Objection | Response structure |
|---|---|
| “Too expensive” | ROI range, cost of delay, phasing |
| “Too risky” | Risk register + mitigations + exit criteria |
| “We tried this” | What is different this time (proof) |
| “Not priority” | Opportunity cost of status quo |
| “Need more data” | Decision with learning plan; cost of waiting |

Place objections in **appendix Q&A**, not main spine—unless one objection blocks the decision.

## Pre-read and live delivery

| Element | Guidance |
|---|---|
| **Pre-read** | 1–2 pages: apex, KMs, ask; send 24–48h before if culture allows |
| **Live** | Do not read slides; state apex in first 60 seconds |
| **Discussion** | Park detail questions to appendix |
| **Follow-up** | Decision log: what was decided, owners, dates |
