# Steering Reporting

## Cadence

| Forum | Frequency | Audience | Focus |
|---|---|---|---|
| Portfolio working | Weekly | DRIs | Blockers, RAID |
| Steering | Monthly | Directors + finance | Decisions, RAG |
| Exec / board | Quarterly | C-suite | Strategy, $, major risks |

## One-page portfolio summary

1. **Headline** — on track / at risk / off track + why
2. **Capacity** — top gaps by region
3. **Spend** — capex YTD vs plan
4. **Milestones** — next 90 days (portfolio-level)
5. **Decisions needed** — numbered, with recommendation
6. **Top 3 risks** — mitigation owner

## Initiative RAG criteria

| RAG | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Green** | Within tolerance on schedule, budget, scope |
| **Amber** | Slip or overrun possible; mitigation active |
| **Red** | Missed gate, critical path broken, or safety/compliance |

## Decision log (append each steering)

| Date | Decision | Rationale | Deferred items |
|---|---|---|---|

## Metrics dashboard (examples)

- Portfolio capex forecast vs actual
- kW and GPU supply vs demand by region
- Initiatives by stage gate
- PUE / sustainability index (portfolio average)
- Major contract renewals in next 12 mo

## Comms quality

- Use `communication-lead` for external or board-facing narrative polish
- Separate **facts** from **asks** — decisions in bullet 5, not buried in status

## Anti-patterns

- Site-level MEP detail in steering deck
- Re-prioritizing every month without new data
- No recorded decision when initiatives defer
