## Workstream framing

When an associate **leads** an actuarial workstream, define upfront:

1. **Decision** — e.g., Q3 reserve estimate, renewal indication, assumption update
2. **Basis** — Statutory, GAAP, IFRS 17, management
3. **Cutoff** — Data date, model version, assumption set ID
4. **RACI** — Analyst tasks vs associate judgment vs fellow sign-off
5. **Milestones** — Data receipt, draft exhibits, peer review, governance meeting
6. **Deliverables** — Memo sections, exhibit index, presentation depth

Document in a one-page **workstream charter** referenced by all workpapers.

## Reserving cycle leadership

Associate lead responsibilities:

| Phase | Associate actions | Delegate to analyst |
|---|---|---|
| Planning | Method plan, segments, prior issues | Pull triangles |
| Diagnostics | Review factor stability, tail, cats | Build diagnostic charts |
| Selection | Choose methods/factors with rationale | Scenario runs |
| Review | Reasonability vs pricing, PYD bridge | Roll-forward tables |
| Close | Draft memo technical sections | Tie-outs to GL/schedules |

Escalate to fellow when:

- **Methodology change** or large tail movement
- **Opinion-year** implications
- **Regulatory** or **audit** challenge on prior position

Deep triangle mechanics → `actuarial-analyst`; opinion narrative → `actuary`.

## Pricing cycle leadership

| Phase | Associate actions | Delegate to analyst |
|---|---|---|
| Scoping | Segments, constraints, targets | Experience pulls |
| Analysis | Credibility/trend judgment | A/E tables |
| Indication | Central recommendation with sensitivities | Exhibit formatting |
| Governance | Present to underwriting/finance | Log constraints |
| Documentation | Methodology memo | Support schedules |

Distinguish **technical indication** from **implemented rate** (commercial constraints documented, not hidden).

Product/legal terms → `property-casualty-insurance` / `life-health-insurance`; enterprise assumptions → `assumption-setting`.

## Methodology memos

Recommended structure for associate-authored technical memos:

1. **Executive summary** (short; fellow may rewrite for board)
2. **Purpose and scope**
3. **Data description** and quality assessment
4. **Methods** — selected and alternatives
5. **Results** — central estimate, ranges, key drivers
6. **Sensitivities** — top drivers only (materiality-based)
7. **Governance** — assumption version, model ID, reviewers
8. **Limitations and open items**
9. **Appendix index** — exhibits, data cuts

Associates ensure **one set of definitions** across memo, exhibits, and any deck.

## Peer review

Effective peer review at associate level:

- Use a **written checklist** (method, data, math, narrative consistency)
- Classify findings: **error**, **documentation**, **judgment disagreement**
- Require **response** from author for material items
- Avoid **rubber-stamp** reviews under time pressure
- Recuse when **conflicted**; name alternate reviewer

Finding severity guide:

| Severity | Example |
|---|---|
| High | Wrong formula driving material $ change |
| Medium | Missing sensitivity on key assumption |
| Low | Formatting, cross-reference, typo |

## Stakeholder communication

| Audience | Associate-appropriate content | Escalate |
|---|---|---|
| Finance / FP&A | Bridges, drivers, metric definitions | Strategy without actuarial backup |
| Audit | Factual schedules, walkthroughs | Judgment on contested reserves |
| Regulator (prep) | Exhibit walkthrough, data lineage | Legal interpretation, commitments |
| Underwriting | Indication, segment behavior | Coverage legal wording |
| Board / C-suite | Rarely primary speaker | Fellow/chief for material decisions |

Prepare **Q&A logs** after meetings; assign owners for follow-ups.

## Integration with analysts and fellows

**Analysts:** clear instructions, definition tab, due dates, QA checklist before review.

**Fellows:** early heads-up on material issues; draft memo 48+ hours before sign-off when possible.

**Chiefs / appointed:** only for tier-1 decisions, opinions, regulatory commitments.

Use `actuarial-consulting` framing when the workstream is **client-facing** with SOW and independence rules.
