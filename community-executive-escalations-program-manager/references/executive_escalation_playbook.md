# Executive Escalation Playbook

## When this path applies

- C-suite or board-level customer contact
- Strategic account tagged at-risk in CRM
- Sales or CS exec escalation with revenue threshold
- Partner or investor-referred issue
- Formal complaint threatening contract or public action

## Roles

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| **Program PM** | Cadence, brief, RAID, no dropped threads |
| **Account DRI** (CS/AM) | Relationship, customer comms, commercial context |
| **Technical DRI** | Facts, ETA, workaround—via support/eng |
| **Exec sponsor** (internal) | Approves commitments, joins customer exec calls |
| **Legal / comms** | Review commitments and external statements |

## Executive briefing (one page)

```markdown
# Exec escalation brief — [Account] — [Date]

**Severity:** E1 | **DRI:** Name | **Next update:** Date/time

## Situation (facts)
- What happened; when detected; current state

## Customer impact
- Users affected; workload blocked; ARR / renewal date

## Business impact
- Churn risk; expansion blocked; reference risk; competitive angle

## Timeline
- Key events (bullets, timestamps)

## Actions taken
- Owner: action — status

## Options
1. [Option A] — pros/cons — ETA
2. [Option B] — pros/cons — ETA

## Recommendation
- Preferred option and why

## Ask
- Decision needed from [role] by [date]

## Comms
- Last customer touch; next planned touch; draft message link

## Open risks
- Legal, security, over-promise, dependency
```

## Customer executive call prep

| Item | Owner |
|------|-------|
| Attendee list and roles | Account DRI |
| No-surprise brief to internal exec | Program PM |
| Demo or workaround ready | Technical DRI |
| Pre-approved talking points | Account DRI + comms |
| Forbidden topics (roadmap, legal) | Product + legal |
| Follow-up email draft within 24h | Account DRI |

## Commitment discipline

- **No** engineering dates without eng DRI sign-off
- **No** contractual concessions without legal/deal ops
- **No** feature promises without product DRI
- Log all verbal commits in decision log same day

## Escalation within the company

| If… | Then… |
|-----|-------|
| Missed customer deadline | Notify exec sponsor + program sponsor |
| Legal threat | Legal lead joins within SLA |
| Needs engineering re-org priority | Product + eng VP in war room |
| Customer exec goes above your exec | Brief your exec before response |

## Pair with other skills

- Engineering facts: `support-engineer` escalation package
- Wording: `communication-lead`
- Terms: `commercial-counsel`
