## Kickoff questions

Ask the sponsor:

- What decision will this work inform? By what date?
- What does success look like in 6–12 months (metrics)?
- Who will disagree with the outcome, and why?
- What analyses already exist? What is politically sensitive?
- What is explicitly out of scope?

## Red flags

- No single decision owner
- "Study everything" without prioritization
- Recommendation predetermined before analysis
- No access to data or frontline interviews
- Success metrics undefined

Escalate early; narrow scope or add a discovery phase.
