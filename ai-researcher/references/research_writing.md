## Memo structure

1. Executive summary (5 sentences)
2. Background
3. Methods tried
4. Results (table + figure)
5. Limitations
6. Recommendation

## Peer review checklist

- Claims match evidence
- Baselines fair
- Limitations honest
- Repro steps sufficient for internal replication
