## Question template

```markdown
## Research question
[Specific, falsifiable]

## Why now
[Gap or business driver]

## Success criteria
[Metric + threshold]

## Baselines
[What must be matched]

## Out of scope
[Explicit exclusions]
```

## Hypothesis types

| Type | Example |
|---|---|
| Comparative | Method A beats B on metric M |
| Ablative | Component X contributes ≥ Y gain |
| Scaling | Performance holds at 10× data |
