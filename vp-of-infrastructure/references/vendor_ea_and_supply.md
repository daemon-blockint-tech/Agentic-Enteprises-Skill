## Commercial portfolio

Treat vendors as a **portfolio** — concentration risk, renewal cliffs, switch cost, and alignment to 3–5 year strategy.

Maintain a **renewal calendar** (next 18 months) with owners: procurement, legal, FinOps, technical lead.

## Hyperscaler and EA

| Activity | Owner | VP role |
|----------|-------|---------|
| Usage forecast | FinOps + product | Approve growth assumptions |
| Commit structure | `cloud-economist` | Choose risk/coverage frame |
| EA/MACC negotiation | Procurement + `enterprise-cloud-architect` | Set walk-away and flexibility needs |
| Utilization tracking | `finops-analyst` | Monthly SteerCo if underutilized |

Guardrails:

- Avoid **over-commit** without stabilized usage and exit clauses understood
- Prefer **portfolio mix** (Savings Plans + shorter term) when growth uncertain
- Document **true-up and true-down** triggers before signature

## Colo and hardware

Partner with DC and supply skills:

| Need | Skill |
|---|---|
| Site selection and MW program | `data-center-portfolio-planning-execution-lead` |
| Delivery execution | `senior-data-center-capacity-delivery-manager` |
| GPU/CPU utilization before buy | `data-center-compute-supply-efficiency` |
| Supply chain and logistics | `supply-chain-manager` |

VP approves **multi-year colo and hardware envelope**; specialists run RFPs and acceptance.

## Critical SaaS

Consolidate observability, CI, security scanners, and ITSM where possible.

Require **exit plan** for Tier 0 dependencies — data export, API replacement, contract break cost.

## Negotiation guardrails

Bring to every major deal:

- **Baseline** — current spend/run-rate and alternatives
- **BATNA** — second vendor or defer decision
- **Flexibility premium** — price shorter term or smaller commit if needed
- **Security and residency** — non-negotiables from security partners
- **Post-sign metrics** — utilization, adoption, savings realization owner

Do not negotiate list price without FinOps actuals; do not sign technical annexes without architect review.
