## DD engagement framing

Clarify up front:

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Deal role | Buy-side, sell-side, reinsurance, runoff |
| Materiality threshold | $ and % of reserves / premium |
| Reliance | Investor memo vs management only |
| Timeline | Binding date vs confirmatory phase |
| Access | Full data room vs sample LOBs |

Consulting delivers **structure and communication**; `actuary` executes technical testing.

## Workstreams

| Workstream | Key questions | Typical exhibits |
|---|---|---|
| Reserves | Adequacy, development, one-times, tail | Triangle summary, PYD bridge |
| Pricing | Rate adequacy, segment mix, trend | LR / combined ratio by segment |
| Reinsurance | Treaty economics, collectibility, commutation | Program summary |
| Claims | Case adequacy, large loss, IBNR process | Large loss listing |
| Capital | RBC/Solvency metrics, stress sensitivities | Capital bridge |
| Models | Governance, version, validation status | Model inventory |

Scope LOBs with **premium or reserve weight** first; expand if red flags appear.

## Data room and requests

Standard actuarial **data request list** (tailor):

- Paid and incurred loss triangles (10+ years where available)
- Premium and exposure by segment
- Reinsurance schedules and recoverables aging
- Large loss and catastrophe listings
- Prior actuarial reports, statements of opinion, APAC memos
- Pricing indications and rate filings (overview)
- Model documentation and validation reports

Track **received / partial / missing** in a matrix; escalate gaps to deal lead.

## Red-flag checklist

Investigate when present:

- **Prior-year development** spikes without narrative
- **Reserve releases** driving earnings
- **Methodology changes** near transaction date
- **Large single claims** or litigation not in case reserves
- **Reinsurance disputes** or slow collectibility
- **Data quality** breaks (unreconciled triangles)
- **Inadequate documentation** for appointed actuary opinion
- **Aggressive loss trend** or discount assumptions
- **New products** with thin experience priced below indication

Rate each: **High / Medium / Low** impact and **High / Medium / Low** confidence.

## Findings and reporting

Finding template:

```markdown
### Finding [n]: [Short title]
- **Area:** Reserves | Pricing | Capital | Reinsurance | Models
- **Observation:** [Fact tied to exhibit]
- **Impact:** [Range or directional effect on deal metrics]
- **Recommendation:** [Further work, price adjustment, rep/warranty, none]
- **Evidence:** Exhibit X, data cutoff Y
```

Separate **confirmatory** items resolved before close vs **post-close** monitoring.

Do not provide **investment advice** or **purchase price** recommendations unless explicitly scoped and qualified.

## Post-close and transition

Optional follow-on scopes:

- Opening balance **reserve true-up** support
- **Transition services** (model transfer, assumption alignment)
- **100-day** model validation or reserve committee setup

Hand legal drafting (R&W, escrow) to `commercial-counsel`; hand ongoing reserving to client or `actuary` retainer.
