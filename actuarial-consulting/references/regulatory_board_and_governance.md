## Governance forums

| Forum | Actuarial consulting role |
|---|---|
| Reserve committee | Agenda, pre-reads, decision log |
| Pricing committee | Rate change summary, competitive context |
| Model risk committee | Validation status, open findings |
| Audit committee | PYD, critical audit matters support (factual) |
| Board risk committee | Tail risk storyline, capital sensitivity slides |

Clarify whether the consultant **presents**, **attends**, or **prepares materials only**.

## Regulatory interaction prep (overview)

**Not legal advice.** Consulting supports:

- **Exhibit index** aligned to prior filings
- **Assumption change log** since last examination
- **Talking points** for management (consistent with memo)
- **Workpaper organization** for document requests
- **Mock Q&A** on methods and data period

Escalate to `commercial-counsel` for:

- Interpretation of statutes and regulations
- Privilege strategy
- Enforcement response

Coordinate with client **appointed actuary** on opinion-related topics.

## Board and committee packs

Minimum pack structure:

1. **Cover memo** (1–2 pages): purpose, recommendation, vote needed
2. **Dashboard** metrics: reserve, PYD, pricing, capital (consistent definitions)
3. **Deep-dive appendix** for material movements
4. **Glossary** of actuarial terms for non-specialist directors

Timing: distribute **48–72 hours** before meeting when possible; version all materials.

## Consistency with public disclosure

Before delivery, cross-check:

- Prior **10-K / statutory annual statement** narratives
- **Earnings call** commentary on reserves and cat losses
- **Rating agency** presentation metrics
- **Investor day** slides if transaction-related

Flag **inconsistencies** to CFO and counsel; do not silently change defined metrics.

## Project governance

| Artifact | Owner | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Status memo | Engagement manager | Weekly on active deals |
| RAID log | PM or consultant | Weekly |
| Review log | EQR reviewer | Per draft cycle |
| Decision log | Client sponsor | Per workshop |

Align large engagements with `technical-program-manager` for **cross-workstream** dates only; retain actuarial content ownership in consulting lead.

## Escalation paths

Escalate immediately when:

- **Data integrity** cannot be resolved before deadline
- **Management pressure** to change conclusions without evidence
- **Scope creep** threatens independence or quality
- **Legal privilege** or regulatory subpoena issues arise
- **Material error** discovered in prior signed work

Document escalation in writing; involve firm **risk management** or **quality** function per policy.
