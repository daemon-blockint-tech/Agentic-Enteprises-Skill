## Formation checklist

- [ ] Entity name availability
- [ ] Jurisdiction selection (business rationale documented)
- [ ] Charter/certificate of incorporation
- [ ] Bylaws or operating agreement
- [ ] Initial directors/managers and officers
- [ ] Registered agent
- [ ] EIN and bank account (coordination with finance)
- [ ] Stock issuance or membership interests documented
- [ ] Qualification to do business in other states if required

Tax and transfer-pricing analysis: separate advisors.

## Intercompany

Common pairs:

- Parent–sub management agreement
- IP license between entities
- Cost-sharing or transfer pricing policy alignment

Route commercial terms of external deals to `commercial-counsel`.
