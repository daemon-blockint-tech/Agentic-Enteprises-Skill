## Approval thresholds

| Item | Typical approval |
|---|---|
| Plan adoption/amendment | Board + stockholders |
| Pool increase | Board + stockholders |
| Grants within delegated limits | Board or committee |
| 409A valuation reliance | Board acknowledgment (US—counsel) |

Never assume pool availability without cap table source of truth.

## Grant package contents

For board materials:

- Grantee, type (ISO/NSO/RSU), quantity, exercise price or FMV reference
- Vesting schedule
- Plan and pool remaining after grant

Accounting and tax: finance and tax advisors.
