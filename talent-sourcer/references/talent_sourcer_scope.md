## Purpose

The talent sourcer role is **proactive identification and engagement** of candidates who may not be actively applying. Success is measured by qualified pipeline volume, reply and interest rates, diversity of slates, and clean handoffs—not by closing offers or designing interviews.

## In scope

| Area | Activities |
|---|---|
| Intake | Translate JD and HM notes into ICP, search plan, and channel mix |
| Search | Boolean, X-ray, LinkedIn Recruiter-style filters, GitHub, communities, alumni lists |
| Mapping | Company/team talent maps, competitor pools, market sizing |
| Qualification | Fit tiering, knock-out rules, evidence-backed shortlists |
| Outreach | Personalized sequences, templates, A/B hooks, opt-out handling |
| Pipeline | CRM hygiene, stages, tags, deduplication, activity logging |
| Diversity | Inclusive sourcing plans, diverse slates, bias-aware screening |
| Handoff | Recruiter/HM packages with context and recommended next steps |

## Out of scope

| Area | Route to |
|---|---|
| Interview design, loops, scorecards | `interview-prep`, `interview-system-designer` |
| Offers, negotiation, comp modeling | `draft-offer`, `comp-analysis` |
| HR policy, ER, workforce planning | `hr-business-partner` |
| Sales prospecting, account intel | `lead-researcher`, `account-research` |
| Employer brand campaigns | `cmo-advisor` |
| Full TA program and employer value prop | `talent-acquisition` |
| Req-only pipeline reporting | `recruiting-pipeline` |

## Deliverables

Typical artifacts the sourcer produces:

- **Sourcing playbook** — ICP, booleans, channels, weekly targets
- **Candidate list** — tiered with links and fit notes
- **Talent map** — companies, teams, segments, warm paths
- **Outreach pack** — templates + personalization tokens
- **Handoff brief** — per A-tier candidate for recruiter/HM
- **Metrics snapshot** — funnel counts and conversion rates

## Quality bar

- Every qualified lead has a **public evidence** line (why this person, why now)
- No fabricated credentials; verify title/company from primary profile
- Dedupe before outreach; honor prior contact and do-not-contact
- Slates reflect **diversity goals** as defined by TA/legal—not quotas on protected classes in search filters where prohibited
- Handoff includes risks (visa, level mismatch, competing process) when known

## Ethics and compliance

- Use only **lawful** data sources and platform terms of service
- Do not scrape private data or bypass paywalls/login walls without authorization
- Respect GDPR/CCPA where applicable: document lawful basis for processing candidate data per org policy
- Avoid discriminatory search filters (e.g., age, gender, ethnicity proxies)
- Stop outreach immediately on unsubscribe or explicit decline
