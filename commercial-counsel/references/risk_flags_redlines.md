## Severity guide

| Level | Examples |
|---|---|
| **High** | Uncapped liability; broad IP assignment; unlimited indemnity; exclusive jurisdiction hostile to company |
| **Medium** | Cap below 12 months fees; one-sided termination; broad audit onsite; weak confidentiality survival |
| **Low** | Notice period wording; non-material defined terms |

## Issue log format

```markdown
| ID | Section | Issue | Proposed change | Fallback | Severity | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 8.1 Limitation | Uncapped direct damages | Cap at 12mo fees | 2x fees cap | High | Open |
```

Status: Open / Agreed / Waived / Escalated
