## DPA checklist

- [ ] Roles (controller/processor) match actual flow
- [ ] Processing purpose and categories described accurately
- [ ] Subprocessors: notice, list, objection window
- [ ] Cross-border transfers: approved mechanism named
- [ ] Breach notification: feasible timeline (e.g., 72h) vs internal IR
- [ ] Deletion/return on termination
- [ ] Audit or certification (SOC 2 report) vs on-site audit scope

## Security exhibit

- Avoid "industry best practices" without reference to SOC 2 or defined controls
- Right to audit: cap frequency and scope; prefer report over onsite
