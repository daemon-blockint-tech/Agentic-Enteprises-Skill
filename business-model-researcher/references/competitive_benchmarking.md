## Peer set selection

Include:

- **Direct** — same buyer, same job
- **Indirect** — different product, same budget line
- **Analog** — different industry, similar model (e.g., take-rate marketplace)

Exclude:

- Aspirational giants unless segment-specific SKU compared
- Pre-revenue startups without pricing unless noted as signal only

Aim for 3–7 peers; explain omissions.

## Comparison dimensions

| Dimension | What to capture |
|---|---|
| Positioning | One-line ICP and promise |
| Revenue model | Sub / usage / hybrid / ads |
| Pricing | Public list price, min contract, free tier |
| GTM | PLG, inside sales, field, channel |
| Geography | Where they monetize |
| Scale signals | Revenue, customers, GMV (if public) |
| Strengths / gaps | Vs your subject |

## Evidence tiers

| Tier | Label | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| A | Verified | 10-K, S-1, investor deck with date |
| B | Strong | Pricing page screenshot + date, CEO interview |
| C | Estimate | Analyst guess, back-of-envelope from hiring/reviews |
| D | Unknown | Mark explicitly; do not fill with fiction |
