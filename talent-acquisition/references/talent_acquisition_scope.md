## Purpose

Talent acquisition (TA) owns **full-cycle recruiting operations**: turning approved headcount into hired employees through requisition management, job design, interview process, candidate experience, offer coordination, metrics, and onboarding handoff. Success is measured by quality of hire, time-to-fill, pass-through, diversity of slates, hiring manager satisfaction, and compliance-ready documentation—not by volume of outbound messages alone.

## In scope

| Area | Activities |
|---|---|
| Workforce tie-in | Align reqs to headcount plans, priorities, and team capacity |
| Requisition | Intake, approval chains, req hygiene, aging req management |
| Job design | JDs, leveling alignment, posting packs, realistic requirements |
| Interview process | Loop design, stages, debrief cadence, scorecard coordination |
| Candidate experience | Scheduling SLAs, communication, rejection hygiene, branding in-hire |
| Pipeline ops | ATS stages, fields, SLAs, reporting, recruiter–HM rhythm |
| Offers | Verbal/written coordination, approvals, negotiation support within band |
| Agencies/RPO | SOW, scorecards, slate quality, fee and exclusivity awareness |
| Metrics | Time-to-fill, pass-through, source mix, diversity funnel |
| Onboarding handoff | Start date package to HR/people ops—not full HRIS provisioning |
| Compliance basics | EEO documentation habits, right-to-work checkpoints (not legal advice) |

## Out of scope

| Area | Route to |
|---|---|
| Boolean search, talent maps, outreach at scale | `talent-sourcer` |
| Interview question banks and rubric authoring only | `interview-prep`, `interview-system-designer` |
| Comp benchmarking and band construction | `comp-analysis` |
| Offer letter legal text and employment law | `draft-offer`, `hr-business-partner` |
| Org-wide people strategy and culture programs | `chro-advisor` |
| Employee onboarding, HRIS, payroll setup | `people-operations-specialist`, `onboarding` |
| Req-only status updates without process ownership | `recruiting-pipeline` |
| Marketing employer brand campaigns (non-hiring) | `cmo-advisor`, `brand-strategist` |

## Deliverables

Typical artifacts TA produces:

- **Req brief** — level, location, must-haves, success profile, comp direction, priority
- **Job description pack** — internal + external JD, inclusive language review notes
- **Interview plan** — stages, interviewers, competencies, timing, debrief rules
- **Pipeline report** — stage counts, aging, pass-through, blockers, HM actions
- **Offer summary** — approved band, exceptions, start date, contingencies
- **Agency/RPO scorecard** — slate quality, time-to-submit, conversion, cost
- **Closed-req retrospective** — metrics, lessons, JD/process updates
- **Onboarding handoff** — role context, team, equipment, day-one for new hire

## Quality bar

- Every open req has an **owner**, HM partner, target start, and weekly sync cadence
- JDs reflect **minimum viable** requirements; avoid credential inflation
- Interview loops have **defined competencies** and documented decisions per stage
- Candidates receive **timely** updates; no ghosting beyond org-defined SLA
- Metrics tie to **actions** (fix JD, add interviewer, change source mix)—not vanity dashboards
- Diversity goals pursued through **inclusive process and slates**, not prohibited search filters

## Boundaries vs talent sourcer

| Dimension | Talent acquisition | Talent sourcer |
|---|---|---|
| Primary goal | Hire approved roles end-to-end | Build qualified pipeline via proactive search |
| Owns req lifecycle | Yes—from intake to close | No—hands off interested candidates |
| JD and loop design | Yes | Inputs only (ICP for search) |
| Outreach execution | May coordinate; not core | Core competency |
| Offer and close | Coordinates through HR/legal | Out of scope |
| Metrics | Time-to-fill, pass-through, HM satisfaction | Reply rate, interested rate, handoff quality |

Use **both** skills on hard reqs: sourcer fills top of funnel; TA owns process, HM partnership, and close.

## Ethics and compliance awareness

- Follow org **EEO/OFCCP** documentation practices; do not provide legal interpretations
- Use **structured** interviews and consistent scorecards where policy requires
- Document **business reasons** for rejections at each stage per ATS policy
- Right-to-work and background checks only per **authorized** playbooks and vendors
- Respect pay transparency laws where applicable; coordinate with HR/legal on postings
- Protect candidate PII; share profiles only with interviewers on a need-to-know basis
