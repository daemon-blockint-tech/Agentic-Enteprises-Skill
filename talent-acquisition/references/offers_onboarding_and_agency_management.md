## Pre-close checklist

Before verbal offer:

| Item | Owner |
|---|---|
| Reference checks per policy | Recruiter / HR |
| Background check scope authorized | HR |
| Level and title confirmed | HM + TA |
| Comp within approved band | HR / comp (`comp-analysis`) |
| Start date feasibility | HM + onboarding |
| Visa / sponsorship path if needed | HR / immigration |
| Debrief consensus documented | HM + panel |
| No pending integrity concerns | HM |

Do not extend verbal offer without **written approval** where org requires it.

## Verbal and written offer flow

1. **Verbal offer** — recruiter or HM; confirm enthusiasm and open questions
2. **Comp summary** — base, bonus, equity, benefits pointer (use approved numbers only)
3. **Contingencies** — background, references, proof of work authorization
4. **Expiration** — reasonable deadline (often 3–7 days); extend only with reason
5. **Written offer** — route through `draft-offer` and HR for legal text
6. **Signature tracking** — ATS stage + HRIS trigger on signed

TA coordinates timing; does not invent legal clauses.

## Negotiation guardrails

- Negotiate only within **pre-approved band** or with live approver on call
- Document asks: base, equity, sign-on, title, start date, remote
- Use **tradeoffs** (e.g., sign-on vs base) per comp philosophy
- Escalate out-of-band requests with business justification and market data from `comp-analysis`
- Avoid verbal promises not in written offer

## Counter-offers and competing timelines

When candidate has competing offer:

1. Clarify **decision date** and components (do not pressure)
2. Accelerate internal approvals if justified
3. Present **total rewards** story, not only cash
4. If declining to match, explain respectfully and leave door open per policy
5. Record loss reason in ATS for market intel

Never share other candidates' offer details.

## Agency and RPO management

**Selection:**

- Use approved vendor list; procurement owns contracts
- Match agency specialty to role family

**Ongoing management:**

| Metric | Review cadence |
|---|---|
| Submissions per week | Weekly on active reqs |
| Screen pass rate | Weekly |
| Interview pass rate | Monthly |
| Time-to-submit | Per SLA |
| Fee / cost per hire | Quarterly |
| Diversity of slates | Monthly |

**Termination triggers:**

- Repeated unqualified submissions after written feedback
- Breach of exclusivity or confidentiality
- Ethical violations (poaching employees, misrepresenting role)

## Onboarding handoff package

Send to `people-operations-specialist` or `onboarding` with:

- Legal name, start date, location, work model
- Manager, buddy, team distribution list
- Equipment needs (laptop, access)
- Day-one agenda and first-week goals
- Role summary and 90-day success profile from req
- Visa / relocation flags if any

TA closes req in ATS only after HR confirms onboarding ticket opened.

## Post-close hygiene

- Survey HM on **quality of hire** at 30/90 days where program exists
- Update closed-req metrics: time-to-fill, source of hire, agency fee
- Retrospect: bottlenecks, JD changes for next similar req
- Nurture silver medalists if talent community policy exists
