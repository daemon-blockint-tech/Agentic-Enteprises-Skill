## Handoff email template

```markdown
Subject: Closed — [Customer] — [Opp ID]

**Signed:** [date]
**Effective:** [start]
**ACV / TCV:** [amounts]
**Products:** [SKU summary]
**Billing contact:** [name, email]
**PO:** [number or N/A]
**Special terms:** [SLA, PS, security — or None]
**Docs:** [link to executed folder]
**CS notes:** [onboarding priority, risks]
```

Finance: confirm invoice schedule and revenue start per `senior-revenue-accountant` policy.
CS: trigger onboarding playbook per `customer-ops-specialist`.
