## Threshold examples

Customize to company policy:

| Trigger | Approver |
|---|---|
| Discount > 20% | VP Sales |
| Net 90+ payment terms | CFO delegate |
| Unlimited liability accepted | Legal + CFO |
| Custom SLA uptime | Eng + Legal |
| Annual contract < $10k | Optional fast track |

## Tracking

Log in deal record:

- Approver name, date, channel (email, workflow tool)
- Version of document approved
- Expiry if approval time-bound (e.g., 30 days)
