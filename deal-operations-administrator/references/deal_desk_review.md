## Intake checklist

- [ ] Opportunity linked to correct legal entity customer
- [ ] Bill-to and ship-to / tenant IDs if applicable
- [ ] SKU list matches approved price book
- [ ] Term and auto-renewal match quote
- [ ] Discount within delegated authority or approval attached
- [ ] MSA in force or new MSA path started
- [ ] DPA required? Data types described
- [ ] PS SOW matches SOW template fields (dates, milestones, fees)

## Common rejects

| Issue | Fix |
|---|---|
| Missing MSA | Legal path or link executed MSA |
| Discount without approval | Attach approval email or workflow ID |
| Custom terms in email only | Move to order form or SOW exhibit |
| Wrong entity on order form | Align with CRM account legal name |
