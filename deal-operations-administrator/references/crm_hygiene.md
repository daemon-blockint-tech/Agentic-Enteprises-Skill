## Weekly checks

- Closed-won without executed contract attached
- ACV mismatch > 1% vs order form
- Opportunity open past close date
- Missing primary quote on late-stage deals
- Duplicate opportunities for same renewal

## Stage definitions

Align sales and deal ops on when to move:

| Stage | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Negotiation | Terms discussed; legal may be engaged |
| Contract out | Sent for signature |
| Closed won | Executed docs received |
| Booked | Finance/provisioning acknowledged |

Do not use Closed Won before fully executed PDFs.
