## End-to-end workflow

```
Plan society path (SOA / CAS / both)
    ↓
Map outstanding VEE areas → approved course options
    ↓
Enroll; retain syllabus and materials
    ↓
Complete course; final grade on transcript
    ↓
Obtain official transcript (and translations if needed)
    ↓
Submit via society portal with required attachments
    ↓
Track status; respond to deficiency requests
    ↓
Confirm VEE credit on society record before claiming on résumé
```

## Before enrollment

1. **Gap analysis** — Which VEE areas remain vs degree plan
2. **List verification** — Society-approved course or listed online program for target term
3. **Schedule fit** — Avoid conflict with intensive exam sitting same term unless intentional
4. **Syllabus archive** — Download catalog PDF even for listed courses (renumbering risk)
5. **Advisor sync** — University actuarial coordinator for dual-degree or minor paths

Deliverable for agents: **one-page plan** with course, term, society, and backup option.

## During the course

1. Keep **graded work samples** only if personal study aids—not for society submission unless requested
2. Note **instructor changes** or syllabus revisions mid-term
3. If course content drifts off objectives, escalate to advisor **before** final exam
4. For online async courses, track **proctored exam** requirements if policy relevant

## After grades post

1. Confirm grade meets **minimum** threshold on **official** transcript
2. Request **official transcript** from registrar (electronic vs sealed paper per society)
3. Update society profile **only after** personal verification of grade
4. If course was borderline, consult society on **repeat policy** before re-enrolling elsewhere

## Documentation packet

Typical items societies may require (check live forms):

| Item | Purpose | Common issues |
|---|---|---|
| Official transcript | Proof of course and grade | Wrong student ID; missing term |
| Course syllabus | Topic coverage evidence | Outline too vague; wrong term syllabus |
| Course description / catalog excerpt | Credit hours and level | Graduate/undergrad mismatch |
| Translation + credential evaluation | International study | Incomplete notarization |
| Society submission form | Area claimed, course codes | Wrong VEE bucket selected |
| Payment / fee confirmation | Processing | Receipt not attached |

### Transcript hygiene

- Use **legal name** matching society account
- Include **all institutions** if transfer chain matters
- For in-progress degrees, partial transcripts may be rejected until final

### Syllabus hygiene

- Include **grading scale**, **credit hours**, **textbooks**, **weekly topics**
- Match **term and instructor** on file
- Redact student PII if sharing samples in forums

## Society accounts and tracking

At overview level:

1. Maintain **single primary email** across submissions
2. Record **confirmation numbers** and submission dates
3. Monitor **deficiency letters**—deadlines to resubmit are strict
4. Do not claim VEE complete on LinkedIn until society **record** reflects credit

SOA vs CAS specifics → `soa_cas_submission_and_timing.md`.

## Employer and reimbursement

Agents may outline **process** only:

- Employer tuition benefits often require **pre-approval** and grade proof
- VEE approval is **independent** of employer reimbursement
- Document course as **professional development** vs degree tuition per HR policy

No tax, immigration, or legal advice.
